Idols.gg

2023

Tech Stack

  • MongoDB

  • Express.js

  • React

  • Node.js

A website allowing fans of e-sports to make predictions on upcoming tournament outcomes. The site features login via Discord, a prediction selector (“Pick’ems”) for overall tournament stats and match outcomes, as well as a leaderboard. The site amassed a total of 854 unique users who participated in making predictions.
