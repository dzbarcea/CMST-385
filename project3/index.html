Background

Randall "Randy" Findell (born December 17, 2001), known professionally as Brakence (stylized as brakence), is an American singer from Columbus, Ohio currently signed to Columbia Records and Sony Music. He is known for his hyperpop songs and falsetto vocals.

Brakence began uploading his work to music sharing platform SoundCloud in 2016, and released the EP FifthEnigma onto the site in December of that year. In 2018, he released his debut commercial EP Hypnagogia, which incorporated lo-fi hip-hop beats and emo-style vocals. In 2019, he released the EP Bhavana. In 2020, he released his debut album Punk2, which would be considered his breakthrough release. In December 2022, he released his sophomore album Hypochondriac under Columbia Records and Sony Music. (Wikipedia, 2023)
